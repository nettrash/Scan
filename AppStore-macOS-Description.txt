Scan reads and generates barcodes and QR codes natively on your Mac. No accounts, no sign-in, no trackers, no telemetry. Point your built-in webcam at a code, or use your iPhone as a Continuity Camera for a sharper, hand-held scan. Drag an image or PDF onto the window to decode it offline. Everything happens on-device.

The point of Scan isn't just to decode a code — it's to understand what's in it. Scan a Wi-Fi QR and you get the SSID and a one-click password copy. Scan a SEPA invoice and you get the IBAN, beneficiary, and amount as separate copyable rows. Scan a Bitcoin URI and you get the address, amount, and a hand-off to your wallet. Scan a vCard and one click adds the contact.

SCANNING
• Live-camera scanning with your Mac's built-in webcam or any Continuity Camera
• Drag-and-drop image and PDF decoding via Finder or the Files app
• Symbologies: QR, Aztec, PDF417, Data Matrix, EAN-8/EAN-13, UPC-E, Code 39, Code 93, Code 128, ITF-14, Codabar, GS1 DataBar, microQR, microPDF417

SMART PAYLOAD DECODING
Scan parses dozens of formats into structured, copyable fields:
• Web & messaging — URLs, mailto, tel, sms
• Wi-Fi — SSID, password, security (incl. WPA3 and Passpoint)
• Contacts — vCard 3.0, MECARD
• Calendar — iCalendar VEVENT (timezones, all-day, UTC)
• Authentication — otpauth (TOTP/HOTP)
• Retail — EAN-8/EAN-13/UPC-E/ITF-14
• Crypto — Bitcoin (BIP-21), Ethereum (EIP-681), Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, Dogecoin, Monero, Cardano, Solana, Lightning (BOLT-11), USDC/USDT/DAI stablecoins
• Bank payments — EPC SEPA / GiroCode, Swiss QR-bill, Czech SPD, Slovak Pay by Square, EMVCo (Pix, PayNow, PromptPay, CoDi, DuitNow, QRIS, FPS, NAPAS, NETS), Indian UPI, Bezahlcode, Serbian NBS IPS QR
• Mobile-payment apps — Swish, Vipps, MobilePay, Bizum, iDEAL
• Receipts — Serbian SUF fiscal receipt
• Identity — DigiD, EUDI, OpenID4VC (recognised and routed to your browser; no verification, no claim storage)

GENERATING
A dedicated Generate tab builds codes from structured input:
• Inputs — Text, URL, Contact (well-formed vCard 3.0), Wi-Fi
• Symbologies — QR, Aztec, PDF417, Code 128
• Custom foreground/background colours with WCAG contrast checking
• Error-correction picker and centred logo embedding
• Export to PNG, SVG, or PDF; share via the system share sheet, save to Photos, or copy image and string to the clipboard

HISTORY
• Saved scans persist locally and sync across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Vision Pro via iCloud
• Searchable list with relative timestamps, payload-kind icons, and favourites
• CSV export of your full history
• Per-record detail with editable notes and per-field smart actions

SHARE EXTENSION
• Send images or PDFs to Scan from Finder, Mail, Messages, Safari, or any app with a share sheet
• Up to 10 images or 10 PDFs per share
• Decoding is in-process and fully on-device

PRIVACY
• No accounts, no sign-in, no telemetry, no advertising, no third-party trackers
• All decoding happens on-device
• iCloud history mirroring uses your own private CloudKit container — Apple sees encrypted blobs only

REQUIREMENTS
• macOS 14 Sonoma or later
• A built-in webcam, USB camera, or any Continuity Camera (an iPhone on iOS 16+ signed into the same Apple ID)
